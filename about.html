About Me

I find the sweet spot between user needs, business goals, and technical feasibility. I'm inquisitive, curious, and I enjoy strategy, problem solving, and simplifying complexities. I work fast and I enjoy structure. My passion is what I do for a living. I combine my knowledge of Psychology, my skills in design, and my understanding of UX and technology to build great products.

My Background

I have a bachelor's degree in Psychology. This helps me to understand human behavior, research users needs, and think critically when I observe user behavior and interactions with the products I design. I have also studied at Emily Carr to gain a solid foundation in visual communication, design thinking, branding, and process development.

My studies at BCIT provided me with in-depth technical expertise in industry standard design softwares. I have worked with other designers, developers, and project managers. I know the tools, I know the methods, and I apply UX principles. My ongoing education, persistence, and my dedication has benefited numerous companies.
